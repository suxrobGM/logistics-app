{{ developerName }} provides the {{ appName }} mobile application for truck drivers. You can request deletion of your driver account and personal data at any time, either directly from the app or by contacting our privacy team.

This page describes the steps to take, what data is removed, what we are legally required to retain, and how long the process takes.

{{ sections[1].title }}

The fastest way to delete your account is from inside the {{ appName }} app:

  1. Open the {{ appName }} app and sign in.
  2. Tap the menu (top-left) and go to Settings .
  3. Open Privacy .
  4. Scroll to Delete Account .
  5. Optionally enter a reason, then confirm. The request enters a {{ gracePeriodDays }}-day grace period during which you can cancel it from the same screen.

{{ sections[2].title }}

If you no longer have access to the app, email {{ contactEmail }} from the address associated with your driver account and include:

  • Your full name as it appears on your driver profile
  • The phone number or email used to sign in
  • The name of the carrier / company you drove for

We will verify ownership before processing the request. Verification typically takes 1–3 business days.

{{ sections[5].title }}

Deletion requests follow a {{ gracePeriodDays }}-day grace period :

  • Day 0: You submit the request. Your account is immediately disabled and you are signed out.
  • Days 1–{{ gracePeriodDays }}: You can cancel the request from the Privacy screen (after signing back in) or by emailing us. Nothing is destroyed during this window.
  • After day {{ gracePeriodDays }}: Anonymization runs automatically. The deletion is irreversible from this point.

{{ sections[6].title }}

If you only want to delete specific data (for example, uploaded documents or saved addresses) without closing the account, email {{ contactEmail }} describing what you want removed. You can also export a copy of your personal data from the same Privacy screen before requesting deletion.
