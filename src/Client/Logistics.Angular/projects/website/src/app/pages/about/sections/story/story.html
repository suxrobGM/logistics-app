Logistics TMS was founded in 2024 with a clear goal: give trucking companies the modern software tools they deserve. Too many fleets still rely on spreadsheets, phone calls, and disconnected systems to manage their operations.

We set out to build an all-in-one platform that brings dispatching, load tracking, invoicing, fleet management, and real-time communication into a single, easy-to-use system. From owner operators with a handful of trucks to growing fleets scaling their operations, our platform is designed to grow with you.

With dedicated portals for dispatchers, customers, and drivers, plus integrations with ELD providers, load boards, and payment processors, Logistics TMS helps every part of your operation work together seamlessly.
