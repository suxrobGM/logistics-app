LogisticsX started in 2024 with a simple goal: give trucking companies software that doesn't make them want to throw the laptop. Too many fleets are still running on spreadsheets, phone calls, and a scatter of half-connected tools.

We set out to build a single platform that handles dispatching, load tracking, invoicing, fleet management, and messaging in one place. It works for an owner-operator with a handful of trucks and for fleets that are growing fast.

There are dedicated portals for dispatchers, customers, and drivers, and integrations with ELD providers, load boards, and payment processors — so the parts of the operation that should talk to each other actually do.
