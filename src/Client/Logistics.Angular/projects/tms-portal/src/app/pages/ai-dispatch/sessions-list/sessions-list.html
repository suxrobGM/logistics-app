AI Dispatch

How it works

The AI dispatcher analyzes your fleet state, unassigned loads, truck locations, and driver HOS hours to optimize load-to-truck assignments. In Suggestions mode, it proposes assignments for your review — approve or reject each one. In Autonomous mode, it executes assignments immediately. The agent checks HOS compliance before every assignment and minimizes deadhead miles by matching loads to the closest available trucks.
